Xenoarchaeology

2016

Xenoarchaeology is a first person adventure-puzzle game that has players exploring the remains of a dead civilization on a remote planet. I created this game as a school project to learn 3D game development in Unity. Asset creation and game logic coding took about five days of work and level design took three days of work.
